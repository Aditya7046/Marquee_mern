introduction

Parul University is a premier multidisciplinary private university located in Vadodara, Gujarat. Established in 2015, it spans a lush 117+ acre campus and is one of India's youngest institutions to achieve a NAAC A++ accreditation. Home to over 70,500+ students—including 6,000+ international students from 75+ countries—it offers 200+ programs across 36 institutes
